## COMMON FUNDER-SPECIFIC ADAPTATIONS

### Federal Grants (SAMHSA, CDC, DOL)
- **Emphasis**: Evidence-based practices, national impact potential, regulatory compliance
- **Required Elements**: Data management plan, dissemination strategy, cultural competency plan
- **Budget Considerations**: Federal fringe rate calculations, travel restrictions, equipment limitations

### State/Regional Grants
- **Emphasis**: Local community impact, state-specific regulations, coordination with state agencies
- **Required Elements**: State certification compliance, coordination letters, local matching funds
- **Budget Considerations**: State wage scales, regional cost variations, local partnership requirements

### Foundation Grants
- **Emphasis**: Mission alignment, innovation, community engagement, measurable outcomes
- **Required Elements**: Theory of change, sustainability plan, community leadership development
- **Budget Considerations**: Administrative cost limits, program focus requirements, reporting expectations

### Corporate/Private Grants
- **Emphasis**: Workforce development, community investment, measurable ROI, brand alignment
- **Required Elements**: Business case, employer engagement, economic impact analysis
- **Budget Considerations**: Performance milestones, cost-per-outcome targets, partnership opportunities
