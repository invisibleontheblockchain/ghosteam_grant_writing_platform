### **1. FUNDING AMOUNT - MAJOR MISCALIBRATION**
- **AI Demo**: $247,500 (3x overestimate)
- **Actual Winner**: $81,753
- **Gap Analysis**: 203% funding overestimate
- **Root Cause**: No funder capacity calibration algorithm
- **Learning**: Region 16 operates in $70-90K range, not $200K+ scale

### **2. PROGRAM SCOPE - OVERENGINEERING**
- **AI Demo**: 35 peer specialists, 8 events, Weld County expansion
- **Actual Winner**: 30 peer specialists, 4 events, El Paso County focus
- **Gap Analysis**: 17% participant overestimate, 100% event overestimate
- **Root Cause**: No scope scaling to budget constraints
- **Learning**: Funders prefer focused, realistic proposals over ambitious expansions

### **3. GEOGRAPHIC TARGETING - WRONG REGION**
- **AI Demo**: Weld County (completely incorrect)
- **Actual Winner**: El Paso County (actual service area)
- **Gap Analysis**: 100% geographic mismatch
- **Root Cause**: Failed to parse organizational service area limits
- **Learning**: Must validate expansion feasibility against existing operations

### **4. TIMELINE ACCURACY - WRONG FUNDING CYCLE**
- **AI Demo**: January 2026 - December 2026
- **Actual Winner**: Q1 2025 - Q1 2026
- **Gap Analysis**: 12-month offset from actual grant period
- **Root Cause**: Misread RFP timeline requirements
- **Learning**: Grant periods are fixed, not projected future dates

### **5. BUDGET STRUCTURE - COMPLEXITY MISMATCH**
- **AI Demo**: 5-category complex breakdown with high overhead
- **Actual Winner**: Lean 4-category structure with essentials focus
- **Gap Analysis**: Overcomplicated financial presentation
- **Root Cause**: No funder preference profiling for budget presentation
- **Learning**: Region 16 prefers straightforward, minimal overhead budgets
